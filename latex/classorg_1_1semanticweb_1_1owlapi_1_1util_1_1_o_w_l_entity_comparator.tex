\subsection{Detailed Description}
Author\-: Matthew Horridge\par
 The University Of Manchester\par
 Bio-\/\-Health Informatics Group\par
 Date\-: 15-\/\-Jun-\/2007\par
\par
 

A {\ttfamily Comparator} which compares entities. Entities are compared first by their type (in the following order\-: Class, Object property, Data property, Individual, Datatype) then by their short form (using the specified short form provider).
