\subsection{Detailed Description}
Author\-: Matthew Horridge\par
 The University Of Manchester\par
 Bio-\/\-Health Informatics Group\par
 Date\-: 26-\/\-Apr-\/2007\par
 \par
 

A mapper which given a root folder attempts to automatically discover and map files to ontologies. The mapper is only capable of mapping ontologies in R\-D\-F/\-X\-M\-L and O\-W\-L/\-X\-M\-L (other serialisations are not supported).
