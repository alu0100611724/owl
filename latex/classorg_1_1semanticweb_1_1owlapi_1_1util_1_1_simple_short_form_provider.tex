\subsection{Detailed Description}
Author\-: Matthew Horridge\par
 The University Of Manchester\par
 Bio-\/\-Health Informatics Group\par
 Date\-: 18-\/\-Apr-\/2007\par
\par
 

A very simple short form provider which is intended to provide human readable display names for entities. The following strategy is used\-: 1) If the entity U\-R\-I has a fragment then that fragment is returned e.\-g. \href{http://an.other.com#A}{\tt http\-://an.\-other.\-com\#\-A} would have a short form of \char`\"{}\-A\char`\"{}. 2) If the entity U\-R\-I does not have a fragment then the last segment of the U\-R\-I path is used e.\-g. \href{http://an.other.com/A/B}{\tt http\-://an.\-other.\-com/\-A/\-B} would have a short form of \char`\"{}\-B\char`\"{}. 3) If the entity U\-R\-I does not have a path then the full U\-R\-I is returned as a string.
