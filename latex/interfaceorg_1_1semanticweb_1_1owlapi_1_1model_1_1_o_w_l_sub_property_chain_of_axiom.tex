\subsection{Detailed Description}
Author\-: Matthew Horridge\par

Represents a \href{http://www.w3.org/TR/2009/REC-owl2-syntax-20091027/#Object_Subproperties}{\tt Sub\-Object\-Property\-Of} axiom in the O\-W\-L 2 Specification where the subproperty is a chain of properties. Note that this axiom type is not explicit in the O\-W\-L 2 specification, but it is included in the O\-W\-L A\-P\-I as a convenience to the programmer.
