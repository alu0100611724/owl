The {\ttfamily \hyperlink{classorg_1_1semanticweb_1_1owlapi_1_1io_1_1_o_w_l_parser_factory_registry}{O\-W\-L\-Parser\-Factory\-Registry}} provides a central point for the registration of parser factories that create parsers to parse O\-W\-L ontologies. The registry is typically used by at least one type of ontology factory for loading ontologies whose concrete representations are contained in some kind of document.
