\subsection{Detailed Description}
Author\-: Matthew Horridge\par
 The University of Manchester\par
 Bio-\/\-Health Informatics Group\par
 Date\-: 08-\/\-Jul-\/2010 

This test case creates a small ontology and tests the getters in the reasoner interface. The test ontology isn't designed to test the correctness of reasoning results, rather it is designed to test the reasoner returns the results in the form required by the O\-W\-L A\-P\-I reasoner interface.
