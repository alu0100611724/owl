\subsection{Detailed Description}
Author\-: Matthew Horridge\par

 Bio-\/\-Health Informatics Group\par
 Date\-: 18-\/\-Dec-\/2006\par
\par
 

Annotations are used in the various types of annotation axioms, which bind annotations to their subjects (i.\-e. axioms or declarations). 

An annotation is equal to another annotation if both objects have equal annotation U\-R\-Is and have equal annotation values
