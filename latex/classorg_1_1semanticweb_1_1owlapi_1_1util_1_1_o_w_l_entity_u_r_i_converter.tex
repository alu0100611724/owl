\subsection{Detailed Description}
Author\-: Matthew Horridge\par
 The University Of Manchester\par
 Bio-\/\-Health Informatics Group\par
 Date\-: 25-\/\-Nov-\/2007\par
\par
 

Performs a bulk conversion/translation of entity U\-R\-Is. This utility class can be used to replace entity names with I\-Ds for example. The entity converter is supplied with a set of ontologies and a conversion strategy. All of the entities that are referenced in the specified ontologies will have their U\-R\-Is converted according the specified conversion strategy.
