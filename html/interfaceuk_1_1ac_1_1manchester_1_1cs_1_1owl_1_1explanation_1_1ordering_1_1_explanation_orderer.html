Gets an ordered (and possibly indented) explanation. The orderer really provides some kind of "presentation" layer to make an explanation easier to read.

Parameters
entailmentThe axiom that represents the entailment that the explanation being ordered is a justification for.
axiomsThe axioms that constitute the unordered explanation
