Detailed Description

Author: Matthew Horridge
The University of Manchester
Information Management Group
Date: 21-Jan-2009

This type of exception is thrown by an org.semanticweb.owlapi.reasoner.OWLReasoner to indicate that the imports closure of the root ontology (returned by org.semanticweb.owlapi.reasoner.OWLReasoner#getRootOntology()) is inconsistent.
