Detailed Description

Author: Matthew Horridge
The University Of Manchester
Information Management Group
Date: 21-Jun-2008

Checks to see if an ontology and its imports closure fall into the OWL 2 DL profile. An ontology is OWL Full if any of the global structural restrictions are violated, if there is punning between object and data properties
