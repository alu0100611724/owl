Detailed Description

Author: Matthew Horridge
The University Of Manchester
Bio-Health Informatics Group
Date: 15-Nov-2006

The OWLParserFactoryRegistry provides a central point for the registration of parser factories that create parsers to parse OWL ontologies. The registry is typically used by at least one type of ontology factory for loading ontologies whose concrete representations are contained in some kind of document.
